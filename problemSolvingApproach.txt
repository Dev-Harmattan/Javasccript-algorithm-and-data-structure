The problem solving approach steps
Step 1) Understand the problem

  1) Can I restate the problem in my own world
  2) What are the inputs that go into problem
  3) What are the outputs that need to come from the solution to the problem
  4) Can the output determine from the input?
  5) How should I label the important pieces of Data that are part of the problem.

Step 2) Explore the concrete example
Step 3) Break it down
Step 4) Solve and Simplify
